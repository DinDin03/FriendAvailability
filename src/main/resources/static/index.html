How LinkUp Works

Get from chaos to coordinated in three simple steps

1. Connect with Friends

Create your profile and connect with friends using their email or by sharing your unique LinkUp code.

2. Share Your Availability

Mark when you're free directly in LinkUp or sync with your Google Calendar for automatic updates.

3. Find Common Time

Our smart algorithm finds overlapping free time slots among your friend group and suggests the best meeting times.
